Our Product

A.I Smart socket:
Experience unparalleled comfort and adaptability with our AI- Smart Socket, featuring dynamic auto-fit technology, advanced moisture control, real-time user feedback, and AI-powered customization—all designed to perfectly fit your lifestyle.

Product Features

  • 1
    Climate control
    Comfort in Every Climate: Our advanced control system actively manages moisture and temperature within the socket, ensuring a dry and cool environment, reducing skin irritation and discomfort, regardless of the weather or activity level.

  • 2
    Auto fit
    Perfect Fit, Every Time: The auto-fit system dynamically adjusts the socket's fit in real-time, responding to changes in limb size and shape, providing a consistently secure and comfortable experience without manual adjustments.

  • 3
    Smart sensing with haptics
    Stay Informed with Real-Time Data: Integrated sensors monitor pressure, humidity, and temperature within the socket, delivering real-time feedback through haptics, allowing users to make instant adjustments for maximum comfort and safety.

  • 4
    Adaptive liner technology
    Comfort That Adapts: Our liner technology absorbs moisture and offers superior cushioning, allowing users to wear their prosthetics comfortably for extended periods. The liner conforms to the shape of the limb, ensuring a custom fit that reduces pressure points and enhances stability.

  • 5
    TRACE Technology
    Easy and Accurate Customization: Our cutting-edge mobile app uses image processing to create a 3D model of your socket simply by taking pictures of your limb. No more lengthy scanning sessions or frequent doctor visits—just snap, send, and receive your custom fit.

  • 6
    Live monitoring
    Comprehensive Dashboard: The app features a detailed dashboard that displays real-time data on humidity, temperature, and pressure within the socket, helping you monitor and adjust your prosthetic for optimal comfort.

  • 7
    Flex mod
    Adaptive Support: The socket automatically adjusts its stiffness based on user activity and environmental conditions, providing the right amount of support when you need it, enhancing both comfort and mobility.

    AI integration

About Us

We are an early-stage startup focused on developing cutting-edge wearable technology solutions for prosthetic and orthotic devices. Currently, we are in collaboration with the I-Hub Foundation for Cobotics at IIT Delhi.

Mission
We at Biokinetic labs envision a future where individuals with disabilities and different abilities are empowered not just to restore their biomechanical functions, but also to enhance and augment their capabilities through innovative robotic solutions.
